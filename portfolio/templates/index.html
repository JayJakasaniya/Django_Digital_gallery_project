let’s introduce My Self

hey, i am jay jakasaniya self taught artist .As a child, I was interested in two things — art and reading.who thought that art was the most important class of the day; who spent hours in the corner drawing. But art did not occur to me as a career option.Learning how to draw is a primary skill that every artist tries to master before diving deep into the creative world. And some of these artists make drawing their only form of creativity, being experts of the pencil technique and challenging the reality of its real object.drawing is probably the most archaic form of expression that is still in use today. It is the simplest way to communicate through visual elements and association, and probably it is the most genuine skill ever. People that draw as an extensive part of their daily lives have a different and more intimate outlook on the world. Think about children, and how they learn how to draw and assemble shapes, colours and lines before they know how to communicate properly. Drawing is indeed an integral part of our communicative beings. Drawing may be an intuitive skill when needed, but also an incredibly challenging skill to master. Instead I got a bachelor’s degree in computer engineering, while painting and drawing in my free time.
